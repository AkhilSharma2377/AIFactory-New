Frequently Asked

How does the model handle regulatory explainability requirements?+
Every decision includes SHAP feature attributions, confidence intervals, and a plain-language rationale — formatted for SR 11-7 Model Risk Management documentation and examiner review.
Which core banking systems can you connect to?+
Pre-built connectors for Temenos T24, Finastra Fusion, FIS, Jack Henry, and Mambu. Salesforce Financial Services Cloud, Bloomberg, and Reuters Eikon are also supported.
How is model bias evaluated for credit decisions?+
We run disparate impact analysis across protected classes per ECOA and Fair Housing Act guidelines before every production deployment, with continuous monitoring for distribution shift.
Can the agents operate in a real-time transaction environment?+
Yes. The fraud and AML agents are designed for sub-100ms inference with horizontal auto-scaling — tested to 50,000 transactions per second.
