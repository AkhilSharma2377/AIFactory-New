Frequently Asked

Which core insurance systems do you integrate with?+
Native connectors for Guidewire ClaimCenter, PolicyCenter, and BillingCenter; Duck Creek Claims and Policy; Majesco ClaimVantage; and Sapiens. ISO/ACORD data standards supported natively.
How do you handle state DOI model governance requirements?+
Every AI model ships with a model card, bias analysis, back-testing results, and an explainability report formatted for state DOI examination and NAIC AI governance framework review.
Can the fraud detection agent handle organized fraud rings?+
Yes. The SIU agent uses graph analytics to surface connected entities across claims, policies, and claimants — detecting rings that single-claim rules miss entirely.
What claim types can be fully automated?+
Straightforward property (hail, wind, water), auto (glass, minor collision), and workers comp medical-only claims are ideal for straight-through processing. Complex injury and liability claims route to adjusters.
