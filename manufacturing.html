Frequently Asked

Does this require replacing our existing SCADA or MES systems?+
No. AI Factory connects to your existing historian (OSIsoft PI, Ignition, Wonderware), MES (SAP ME, Siemens Opcenter), and ERP via secure API or OPC-UA adapters.
How are models trained without sending our data to the cloud?+
Initial model training uses federated learning or on-premise compute. Your production data, process recipes, and quality specs never leave your facility network.
What sensor types and industrial protocols are supported?+
OPC-UA, MQTT, Modbus, PROFINET, and EtherNet/IP. Standard historian tags from OSIsoft PI, Ignition, and Aveva are supported natively.
Can the quality inspection agent work at full production line speed?+
Yes. The visual inspection agent runs on edge GPUs at up to 120 parts/minute with sub-20ms inference latency — certified for ISO 9001 quality management integration.
