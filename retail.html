Frequently Asked

Which commerce platforms and OMS systems do you integrate with?+
Pre-built connectors for Shopify, Salesforce Commerce Cloud, Magento, SAP Retail, Oracle Retail, and Manhattan OMS. Custom integrations via REST API ship in 1–2 weeks.
How do you handle cold-start for new customers?+
New visitor recommendations use collaborative filtering bootstrapped from segment-level data. Accuracy reaches 85% of returning-user quality after just 3 sessions.
Can the demand forecasting agent handle promotional events and seasonality?+
Yes. The model is trained on your full promotional calendar, seasonal indices, and external signals like weather and school holidays.
How does dynamic pricing stay compliant with MAP policies?+
MAP floors, promotional windows, and competitor price floors are configurable constraints — the agent never breaches guardrails you set.
