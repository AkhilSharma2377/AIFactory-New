Frequently Asked

Is PHI ever used to train shared models?+
No. AI Factory deploys inside your tenant and uses retrieval — not fine-tuning on your PHI. All prompts, retrievals, and responses stay within your cloud boundary.
Which EHRs and data sources are supported?+
Native connectors for Epic, Cerner, Athena, and MEDITECH via FHIR R4 and HL7 v2. Claims (837/835), DICOM, lab (LOINC), and pharmacy (NCPDP) supported out of the box.
How is clinical accuracy evaluated?+
Every agent ships with board-certified specialist review, adversarial red-team prompts, bias audits across demographics, and continuous drift monitoring in production.
What does a typical deployment timeline look like?+
Discovery (1 wk), pilot config (2–3 wks), clinical validation (2 wks), limited production (2 wks), scaled rollout. Most customers reach first production agent in 6–8 weeks.
Do you sign a Business Associate Agreement?+
Yes. A BAA is executed before any PHI touches the platform. AI Factory is HIPAA, HITRUST r2, and SOC 2 Type II attested.
